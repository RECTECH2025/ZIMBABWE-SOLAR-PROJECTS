Zimbabwe Solar Park Projects

Addressing the National Energy Deficit

Zimbabwe faces a significant power deficit, with an installed capacity of 2,100MW but generating only 1,200MW to 1,300MW, resulting in an **800MW deficit**.

To tackle this challenge, local investors have established utility-scale Photovoltaic (PV) solar firms, bringing much-needed renewable energy to the national grid. This presentation summarizes three key projects driving this initiative.
